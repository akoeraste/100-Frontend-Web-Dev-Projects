# 002 - Tribute Page

A tribute to **DJ Arafat** (Ange Didier Houon) — the King of Coupé-Décalé. Built with a dark, gold-accented musical aesthetic to honour one of Africa's most influential artists.

## Preview

![Tribute Page — DJ Arafat](../../images/002.png)

## Features

- **Cinematic hero** with concert background, gold particle effects, and animated equalizer bars
- **Spinning vinyl record** next to the artist bio — CSS-only animation
- **Interactive timeline** of DJ Arafat's life and career milestones
- **Legacy cards** highlighting his impact across music, culture, and performance
- **Discography section** with track list and animated popularity bars
- **Stat counters** with scroll-triggered number animation (200+ songs, 30+ awards, etc.)
- **Memorial section** with French quote, heartbeat icon, and radial glow
- **Scroll-triggered reveals** with staggered timing throughout
- **Fully responsive** for mobile, tablet, and desktop

## How to Run

Open `index.html` in any browser. No build tools or dependencies required.

## Sections

1. **Hero** — Full-viewport cinematic intro with name, dates, equalizer, gold particles
2. **The Legend** — Bio with spinning vinyl record and cover art
3. **The Journey** — Vertical timeline from 1986 to 2019
4. **The Legacy** — Four cards: The Sound, The Energy, The Movement, The Influence
5. **Discography** — Eight iconic tracks with animated popularity bars
6. **Numbers** — Animated stats (200+ songs, 15+ albums, 30+ awards, 1M+ lives touched)
7. **Memorial** — Closing tribute with French quote, heartbeat, and farewell message
8. **Footer** — Navigation, tribute text, and crown icon
